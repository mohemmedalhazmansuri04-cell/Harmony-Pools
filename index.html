About Harmony Pools

Rick Jank has been working in the pool industry for nearly four decades. His journey began over 39 years ago, transforming backyards into beautiful oasis.

In 2017, Rick started Harmony Pools to deliver quality workmanship, lasting results, and customer satisfaction on every project.

“What I enjoy most is seeing the smile on a customer’s face when the job is complete. That satisfaction motivates me every day.”

Rick’s goal remains the same — to create pools and outdoor spaces where families can relax, make memories, and enjoy for years to come.
